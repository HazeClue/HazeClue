## ‚ö° What is tDCS?

tDCS = Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation
A non-invasive technique using low electrical current to stimulate specific brain regions through the scalp.
Helps improve focus, memory, and mood, and is also used in neuro-rehabilitation.

‚úÖ Safe Usage:

* Session duration: 20‚Äì30 minutes maximum.
* Current: 0.5‚Äì2 mA only.
* Not for children or epilepsy patients without specialist supervision.
* Electrodes must be well-moistened to reduce skin resistance.
* Stop immediately if any discomfort occurs (headache, skin irritation).
